## Sub-maintainers

Sub-maintainers own a specific area but do not have a vote on protocol-level decisions outside their area.

| Area                        | Maintainer | Notes              |
|-----------------------------|------------|--------------------|
| `bindings/rust/`            | _vacant_   | Rust crate         |
| `bindings/typescript/`      | _vacant_   | npm package        |
| `integrations/platformio/`  | _vacant_   | PlatformIO library |
| `integrations/esphome/`     | _vacant_   | ESPHome component  |

To volunteer for a sub-maintainer role, open an issue tagged `maintainer-volunteer`.
